🛡Welcome to HalfXP!🛡

The newbie-friendly Alliance guild on Icecrown!

We're focussed on levelling, questing, addons, lore, questions you have. In other words: PvE and Social stuff.

No end-game stuff (though we're not against it); Dungeons are fine :)

❓How to invite people

We don't do that by hand; here's a macro:

        /script n=GetNumWhoResults(); i=1; while(i<n+1) do c, g=GetWhoInfo(i); if(g=="") then SendChatMessage("Hi, "..c.."! HalfXP is a newbie-friendly guild for people who play with 0.5x XP, or just play casually; Care to join?", "WHISPER", "COMMON", c); GuildInvite(c); end; i=i+1; end;

Source: https://us.forums.blizzard.com/en/wow/t/is-there-a-way-to-shoot-a-mass-invite-out-for-guild-recruitment/544867/2

👶I'm new, how do I guild?

Press the o key to open the social window, and find the guild tab and the bottom.

🤔Why join?

So you have a place to interact with a bunch of nerds if you want to know more about the game, be it addons, lore, question, gear, etc.
